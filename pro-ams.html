NTU PRO-AMS DEBATE TOURNAMENT

NTU Pro Ams is an inter-tertiary debating championship organized by the NTU Debating Society. NTU Pro Ams aims to give novice speakers exposure to the world of debating, at an appropriate level of competitiveness. It serves as a platform for speakers to gain familiarity with the British Parliamentary style of debating, under the guidance of experienced debaters. As the World Universities Debating Championship (WUDC) is also held in the British Parliamentary style, experienced debaters also have opportunities to brush up their skills.

The tournament witnesses an annual participation of over 100 debaters from the local Junior Colleges, Polytechnics and Universities and at the same time attracts a substantial pool of talent and high IQ individuals from abroad. The tournament gives the speakers the opportunity to connect and network with experienced speakers from different institutions and backgrounds. It felicitates in the sharing of ideas and knowledge amongst the speakers.
