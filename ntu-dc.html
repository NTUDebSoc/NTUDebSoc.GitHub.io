NTU DOROTHY CHEUNG DEBATING CHAMPIONSHIP

NTU Dorothy Cheung Debating Championship is a highly reputed international debating tournament organized by the NTU Debating Society. For the past few years, the NTU Dorothy Cheung has been seen as one of Singapore’s biggest and finest debate tournaments, serving as an ideal platform for the culmination of some of the best debates in the circuit. Conducted in the Asian Parliamentary format, it provides invaluable experience in the lead up to the biggest tournaments of the season.

Every year it attracts over 300 debaters from the local Junior Colleges, Polytechnics and Universities as well as a substantial pool of talent and high IQ individuals from universities abroad, like UiTM, MMUM, etc.

The tournament also gives speakers the opportunity to connect and network with participants from different institutions and backgrounds. It facilitates in the sharing of ideas and knowledge amongst the speakers.

NTU Dorothy Cheung Debating Championship 2016

NTU Debating Society is proud to present NTU Dorothy Cheung Debating Championship 2016. The event will be held on 25th, 26th and 27th March 2016. The debate will follow the Asian Parliamentary debating format, which is a 3-on-3 debate format. The team cap for this tournament is 72 teams. Adjudicator registrations will be following the N1 rule.

We believe that the NTU Dorothy Cheung Debate Championship 2016 will be an ideal preparatory tournament to Bali UADC 2016 and SolBridge Australs 2016. Scheduled at a time when most institutions would have formed their UADC squads, NTU DC's 2016 will be the perfect avenue and offer an opportunity for debaters to acclimatise themselves to the debate circuit in the region. Considering the tournament’s grand success over the past few years and it serving as a precursor to UADC, we are sure that Dorothy Cheung Debate Championship 2015 will be even bigger and better than ever before.
