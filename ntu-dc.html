NTU DOROTHY CHEUNG DEBATE TOURNAMENT

NTU Dorothy Cheung Debating Championship is a highly reputed international debating tournament organized by the NTU Debating Society. For the past few years, the NTU Dorothy Cheung has been seen as one of Singapore’s biggest and finest debate tournaments, serving as an ideal platform for the culmination of some of the best debates in the circuit. Conducted in the Asian Parliamentary format, it provides invaluable experience in the lead up to the biggest tournaments of the season.

Every year it attracts over 300 debaters from the local Junior Colleges, Polytechnics and Universities as well as a substantial pool of talent and high IQ individuals from universities abroad, like UiTM, MMUM, etc.

The tournament also gives speakers the opportunity to connect and network with participants from different institutions and backgrounds. It facilitates in the sharing of ideas and knowledge amongst the speakers.
